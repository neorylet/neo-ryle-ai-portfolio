## California Housing Clustering

#### Overview
Applied K-Means clustering to group housing data based on geographic location and house value.

---

#### Process
- Selected features: longitude, latitude, median_house_value  
- Applied StandardScaler  
- Trained K-Means model with three clusters  
- Evaluated using Silhouette Score  
- Visualized clusters  

---

#### Results
Clusters represented regional groupings:
- Northern region  
- Central region  
- Southern region  

---

#### Insights
- Clustering revealed geographic segmentation  
- Moderate silhouette score reflects overlapping real-world data  
- Useful for identifying housing trends across regions
